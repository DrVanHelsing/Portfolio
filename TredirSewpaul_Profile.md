### Structural Geology Field Mapping Simulator
**Browser-Based 3D Educational Tool**

A browser-based 3D interactive geology simulator for education, training, and research. Features a procedurally generated 2 km × 2 km terrain with geologically accurate stratigraphic layers, hydraulic erosion, real-time water rendering, and a full suite of virtual field instruments.

**Highlights:**
- 148K-vertex procedural terrain carved by 38,000 hydraulic erosion iterations
- 6 stratigraphic layers (Precambrian to Quaternary) with realistic fault and fold structures
- Unified PBR water rendering: Gerstner waves, Fresnel-Schlick, subsurface scattering, caustics
- Virtual field instruments: borehole drilling, strike & dip measurement, cross-section generation, elevation sampling
- 9,000+ instanced vegetation objects, 600+ riparian plants, full farm compound with windmill
- Physical atmospheric scattering sky dome with animated clouds and Rayleigh/Mie scattering

**Technology Stack:**
`Three.js` · `WebGL` · `JavaScript` · `GLSL` · `Node.js`
